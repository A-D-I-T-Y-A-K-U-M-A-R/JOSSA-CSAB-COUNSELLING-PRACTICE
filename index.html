JoSAA Counselling FAQ

What is JoSAA Choice Filling?
JoSAA choice filling is the process where JEE qualified students select their preferred IIT, NIT, IIIT and GFTI institutes during counselling.

Why use a JoSAA simulator?
A simulator helps students test their preference order before the real JoSAA counselling begins.

Who can use this simulator?
Any JEE aspirant preparing for JoSAA counselling can use this tool to practice choice filling.
